§  FAQ

Common questions

Is Audity really self-hosted?

Yes. Audity runs entirely on your own infrastructure as a Docker stack — on any IP or domain, with no forced outbound calls. There is no cloud requirement.

How is the chain of evidence tamper-evident?

Audit and activity logs are append-only, every entry is hash-chained, and the archive is signed (WORM). Any later change provably breaks the chain.

How does licensing work?

Free needs no license. Pro and Enterprise are unlocked with a locally verified key — your data never leaves your server. Get in touch and we'll set you up.

Can I move between plans later?

Any time. Tiers are cumulative — upgrading unlocks the extra scope instantly, and your existing data is untouched. On downgrade, your data stays and remains exportable.
